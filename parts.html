Computer Parts Selection

How do I select my parts?

As a first time builder, there are several factors to consider when selecting computer parts for your build. Firstly, determine the purpose of your build - whether it's for gaming, productivity, or a mix of both. This will help you select the appropriate processor, graphics card, and RAM.

For gaming, it's recommended to invest in a high-end graphics card, such as the Nvidia RTX 3070 or AMD Radeon RX 6800. Pair it with a powerful CPU, such as the Intel Core i7-11700K or AMD Ryzen 7 5800X, and at least 16GB of RAM.

For productivity, focus on a CPU with a high core count, such as the AMD Ryzen 9 5900X or Intel Core i9-11900K, and at least 32GB of RAM. You may also want to consider a dedicated graphics card if your tasks involve video editing or 3D rendering.

In terms of other components, consider investing in a high-quality power supply unit (PSU) with a wattage appropriate for your build, a reliable motherboard with the necessary features for your needs, and a sturdy case with adequate ventilation.

When selecting parts, make sure to check their compatibility with each other, using tools such as PCPartPicker or BuildMyPC.
